About us

High Speed, Low Latency Internet for Everyone

Golden WiFi, powered by Golden Internet Services Limited, is an NCC-licensed provider redefining how communities across Nigeria connect to the internet.

We operate as a nationwide hotspot network, partnering with major internet carriers to deliver fast, reliable, and truly unlimited WiFi access at an affordable rate.

Our solution is simple yet powerful. By installing smart WiFi devices in hostels, residential areas, and community hubs, we extend enterprise-grade connectivity directly to the people who need it most.

From students and families to small businesses, Golden WiFi removes the high cost and complexity of fiber installations, offering seamless access without data caps or limitations.

Committed to regulatory compliance, innovation, scalability, and inclusion, we are building the foundation of Nigeria’s next digital revolution, one hotspot at a time.

Our Roadmap

Building the Future of Connectivity

At GOLDEN WIFI, our mission is clear. To make unlimited internet access affordable, reliable, and available to every student across Nigeria.

We are not rushing to grow overnight; we are building a strong foundation, one community at a time.

PHASE 1

Growth and Community Expansion

We are now expanding to cover more hostels within Malete and nearby areas, working directly with landlords and hostel agents to integrate GOLDEN WIFI into their buildings at no cost.

Our focus is to build trust locally, perfect the technology, and strengthen our support system before expanding to other cities.

By the end of this phase, our goal is to cover major student clusters in Kwara State and establish GOLDEN WIFI as the preferred hotspot brand for students.

PHASE 2

Nationwide Rollout and Smart Integration

Our long-term vision is to expand across all major university cities in Nigeria, from Lagos and Abuja to Port Harcourt and Kano.

Beyond providing WiFi, we aim to integrate digital learning support, student-friendly data analytics, and campus innovation tools, turning GOLDEN WIFI into more than a hotspot. A digital ecosystem for students and communities.

PHASE 3

Foundation (Completed and Active)

We began our journey in Malete, Kwara State, by setting up our first solar-powered network hub and hotspot base stations across student hostels.

This phase focused on testing coverage, user experience, and reliability to ensure smooth performance in a real-world environment.

It proved that our model works. Students want affordable, flexible, and unlimited access right in their hostels.

PHASE 4

Regional Expansion

Once our Malete network reaches full capacity, we will expand into other university communities, starting with Ilorin, Osogbo, and Ibadan, where demand for reliable student internet is already rising.

This phase includes deploying additional solar-powered access points, improving our monitoring systems, and setting up a regional technical team to ensure consistent service and uptime.
